Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book a virtual consultation?

Yes, HealthBridge Initiative allows you to schedule virtual consultations with certified healthcare professionals through our video call feature. Simply choose a time slot that works for you.

Locating healthcare centers?

You can use our map locator feature to search for nearby healthcare centers based on your location. You can also filter by the type of care you need, such as specialists or primary care.

What healthcare plans are available?

We offer a range of flexible health insurance plans, from basic coverage to more comprehensive packages, designed to fit your specific needs. You can explore and select the plan that best suits you.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. All health data shared on HealthBridge is encrypted, ensuring your personal information remains confidential.

How do I reach the support team?

You can reach out to our friendly customer support team through live chat or email. We’re here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

How can I update my plan?

You can modify your healthcare plan or subscription at any time directly from your user dashboard, ensuring you’re always on the right plan.

What are the benefits of VHA?

With virtual health assistance, you can consult healthcare professionals remotely, saving time and accessing healthcare services conveniently from the comfort of your home.
